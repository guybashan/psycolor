PsyColor Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

PsyColor (“we”, “the app”) is a color personality test app. Your privacy matters to us.

Data we collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server. All app data stays on your device, including:

  • Credit balance
  • Personality test results and color rankings
  • Onboarding preferences

Data storage

Data is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Third-party services

The app may use your device’s share sheet to export your profile text. Sharing is initiated by you and governed by the app you share to.

Future versions may offer in-app purchases through Google Play or Apple App Store, which are subject to those platforms’ privacy policies.

Children

PsyColor is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
